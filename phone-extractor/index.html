What formats are supported?

We capture most common international formats, including numbers with +country code, spaces, parentheses, and dashes.

How do you format to E.164?

Turn on ‘Format E.164’ and choose a default country. Numbers without a leading + will be prefixed with that country code.

Is everything local?

Yes. Your text stays in the browser—nothing is uploaded.
