    FAQ

    Does AIConverter.tools upload my files?

    No. Everything runs in your browser; your data stays on your device unless a tool explicitly says otherwise.

    Is it free?

    Yes. Most tools are free. We support development with clearly labeled ads.

    Can I link directly to a tool?

    Absolutely. Use clean URLs like /csv-to-json, /csv-to-pdf, etc.

    Why am I seeing ads?

    Ads keep the tools free. We follow Google’s guidance on responsible placement and labeling.
