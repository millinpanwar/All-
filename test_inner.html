CSV to JSON — FAQ

          

            Does this upload my CSV?
            
No. Everything runs locally in your browser; your data stays on your device.

          

          

            Can I convert large files?
            
Most browsers handle files up to a few hundred MB. For huge files, split them first.

          

          

            How do I include a header row?
            
Keep “Header row” checked and ensure your first line contains column names.
