How do I reference CSV columns?

Use double braces like {{columnName}}. If your CSV has no header, uncheck the header option and use {{c1}}, {{c2}}, etc.

What filters are available?

Use {{name|upper}}, {{name|lower}}, or {{name|urlencode}} to transform values.

Does this upload my CSV?

No. Parsing and rendering are done entirely in your browser.
